About WorkTree

The NEIS has been helping job seekers become self-employed for a long time. With 8600 new places each year nationally why not see if you can get the training and support to give it a go?

Using the NEIS Data, which contains 53 646, data points WorkTree found a success:failure ratio. This ratio measures how many applicants were successful vs those that failed. When comparing this ratio with the country average, ABS data, you can see that support from the NEIS has proven very useful and successful in many industries.

Using data from the employment projections 2020 you can also find the growing markets for your business to have the best chance of success.
